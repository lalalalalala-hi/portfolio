EcoVista City

  • Sharp3D
  • Reality Composer

EcoVista City is an urban development project designed in alignment with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), emphasising sustainability, inclusivity, and technological innovation. This visionary initiative introduces a 15-minute city concept, prioritising sustainable mobility, green spaces, mixed-income communities, and advanced technologies like IoT and AI.

iNav

  • Unity

iNav is a marker-based AR indoor navigation system designed to guide users through large, complex environments like shopping malls. By leveraging augmented reality, iNav provides clear, real-time directions using visual markers placed throughout the space, ensuring users can easily find their desired locations, vehicles, and emergency exits.
